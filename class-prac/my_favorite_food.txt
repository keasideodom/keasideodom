Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe:
Ingredients:
- 200g spaghetti
- 100g pancetta
- 2 eggs
- 50g pecorino cheese
- 50g parmesan cheese
- Black pepper
- Salt

Instructions:
1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water.
2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
3. Beat eggs with cheese and black pepper.
4. Mix hot spaghetti with pancetta and egg mixture.
5. Serve immediately.
