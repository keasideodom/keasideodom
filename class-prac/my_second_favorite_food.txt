Chicken Tikka Masala Recipe:
Ingredients:
- 500g chicken breast
- 200g yogurt
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 can tomato puree
- 100ml heavy cream
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices for 1 hour.
2. Cook chicken in a pan until golden brown.
3. Saut√© onion and garlic, then add tomato puree.
4. Add chicken, simmer for 10 minutes.
5. Stir in cream, season, and serve with rice....
